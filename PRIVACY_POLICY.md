# Privacy Policy for SlimDesk Navigator

**Last Updated:** January 26, 2026

## 1. Introduction
SlimDesk Navigator ("the App") is a user interface extension for the Frappe Framework. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data you process using our App.

## 2. Data Collection and Storage
**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers.**
*   **Local Processing:** All data processed by the App (including sidebar shortcuts, workspace configurations, and user preferences) is stored directly on your own self-hosted Frappe/ERPNext instance or the server where you have installed the App.
*   **No Telemetry:** The App does not contain any tracking scripts, analytics, or telemetry features that send data to us or third parties.

## 3. Data Usage
The App uses your data solely for the purpose of rendering the sidebar navigation interface within your specific instance.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App functions entirely within the Frappe Framework ecosystem and does not integrate with or send data to any external third-party services.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
